HAVE A ROARING GOOD TIME

The Association for Women in Computing's Casino Night is an annual fundraiser to support women in tech. It will be held on April 18th in the Samuel Riggs IV Alumni Center. Our theme this year is the Roaring 20's, so dress to impress!

Ticket purchases are only accepted through Venmo or cash. At the door, we will only accept venmo.

Ticket Prices:

  • $5 for students
  • $10 day of for students
  • $15 for alumni
Once you arrive at the event with your ticket, you will receive $500 funny money to gamble with your favorite CS professors and TAs. Before the night starts, we have a networking hour from 5-6PM, so feel free to drop by and meet our sponsors and other attendees!

Come and network with our sponsors, enjoy some delicious food, play games with our CS department, and enter your winnings into a raffle for fabulous prizes, such as the grand prize of a Nintendo Switch!
